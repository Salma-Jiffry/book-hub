Pride and Prejudice

Jane Austen

This classic romantic novel, first published in 1813, is one of Jane Austen's most beloved works. It follows the life of Elizabeth Bennet, a sharp-witted and independent young woman, as she navigates issues of manners, upbringing, morality, education, and marriage in the landed gentry of early 19th-century England. The novel is renowned for its lively dialogue and insightful exploration of the nature of relationships and society.
